Our work

The centre is currently involved in numerous projects i.e donating to the needy by donating wheelchairs and providing with food on Mondays and Fridays working with other organizations and is involved in doing some community services in different areas by cleaning up the areas that can be harmful to the community.

With the current projects that the centre is involved in it has been able to employ more young people, with some participating in sports that the centre is also sponsoring which also results in low criminal activities. Some people have also acquired certain skills during these projects doing some handy works, eg doing plumbing work and also some electrical work which also help them to offer their services to some people that need these services.

The centre has also started with helping school kids by having after school programs, helping them with their home works and projects. The centre has had some positive support from the communities, which also helps the centre to grow and can be able to help and empower more people to do good not only for money but for the good and change it brings to peoples lives.


Bring life, Bring change. Together we can do it.
