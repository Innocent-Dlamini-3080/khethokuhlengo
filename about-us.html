About us

KHETHOKUHLE CENTRE is a NGO(Non-Government Organisation) that was established in 2009.
It is a centre that assist people who have no families with food, support groups and
health care, working alongside with other organisations such as Pic’ n Pay and Shoprite as
our sponsors, providing the centre with food and other personal care items. The centre has
been able to employ more people throughout the years as it has grown into something bigger than
it was when it was established, having more sponsors and volunteers assisting the centre
as it has more people who are in need.


KHETHOKUHLE CENTRE was discovered by INNOCENT DLAMINI a young man who had no family left but
was driven by hunger to do more in life not just for himself but for other people. The aim of
the centre is to help people who are in need and don’t have anyone to take care of them and also to
empower them. The centre is hoping to build more facilities in the future to cater for more people
in different regions hoping to archive more with the help of sponsors and people who are willing to assist by
volunteering and by donating with what they have.
